# Privacy Policy for AI Destiny Master

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Destiny Master (hereinafter referred to as "this Application"). This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

## 2. Data We Collect

This Application collects the following types of data:

### 2.1 User Input Data
- Birth date and time (for destiny calculation)
- Names (for compatibility matching feature)

### 2.2 Locally Stored Data
- Calculation history records (including calculation results and related input information)
- Application language preference settings

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data only for the following purposes:

- To provide destiny calculation and compatibility analysis services
- To save your calculation history within the Application for your convenience
- To optimize application performance and user experience
- To provide localized services based on your language preferences

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- All user input data is stored only locally on your device (using browser localStorage)
- We do not upload your personal data to any external servers
- Application developers cannot access or view data stored on your device
- You can delete your calculation records at any time through the history management feature in the Application

## 5. Third-Party Services

This Application uses the following third-party services:

### 5.1 Google Gemini API
- Purpose: To provide AI destiny calculation and compatibility analysis functionality
- Data Processing: User-input birth dates and names are temporarily sent to Google Gemini API for processing
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### 5.2 Other Third-Party Resources
- Tailwind CSS CDN (for application styling)
- Google Fonts (for application typography)
- These services do not collect or store your personal data

## 6. User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

- To view and delete your calculation history at any time
- To change application language preferences
- To clear browser cache to remove all locally stored data
- To stop using this Application and delete all related data

## 7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be published within the Application or on relevant platforms. We recommend that you review this policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
